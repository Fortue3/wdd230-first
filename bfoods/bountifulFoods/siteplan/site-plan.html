Overview

Purpose

To provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal area. Also Promote healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience. Increase the business's online presence.

audience

The local population and tourists in the coastal area who enjoy our products, have access to internet, wish to order online and have an an oppotunity to mkae their fruit mix.

Personas

Dian is a mom who has to be up early to take the care of her children and get them to school on time. Due to this she does not have much time to do shopping. She orders from the Bountiful Foods website in bulk and has fresh fruits delivered to her door step, this saves her a lot of time and does not affect her daily activity. Tafadzwa is a young man working in the field of web development. He has to work remotely but still desires a morning fruit drink to get his day going. He orders from the Bountiful Foods online website for delivery to his current work space.
